About

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the PhysicoChimie des Processus de Combustion et de l'Atmosphère Lab at the University of Lille. I am working towards the development of laser spectroscopic diagnostics to obtain quantitative measurements of several key species for understanding NO formation in laminar and turbulent H2 flames.

In my previous life, I earned my M.Tech. and Ph.D. in the area of thermoacoustic instability. During my Masters and PhD, I worked with R. I. Sujith to study coupled thermoacoustic systems through dynamical systems theory. Before graduate school, I worked at Exide Industries Ltd. as a Production shift in charge of the Boxing Section. I received my Bachelor in Mechanical Engineering from Birla Institute of Technology, where I was advised by Dr. Apurba Kumar Roy.
